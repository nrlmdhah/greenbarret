About Us

Committed to Safety. Driven by Excellence.

GREEN BARRET FORCE SDN BHD (1060943-W) has expanded its security services in line with industry growth, focusing on delivering reliable, high-quality, and high-value support. The company is committed to providing dependable services that meet customer needs in an increasingly challenging global environment.

To ensure fast and dependable service, the company upholds strict human resource practices, enforces strong safety standards, and provides intensive training for all security personnel. All guards are thoroughly vetted by the Royal Malaysian Police (PDRM). Backed by solid industry experience, GREEN BARRET FORCE SDN BHD proudly serves clients across the industrial, manufacturing, telecommunications, and corporate sectors.
